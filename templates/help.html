Healthy Meal Planner is a application to aid diet planning. It is based on the guidelines for a 1800 calorie intake per day of Live Health Online.

Just create an account and you are ready to build healthy balanced meal plans.

How to Register

  1. From the navigation bar, go to the Login / Register page.
  2. Enter your unique username and password and click Submit.
  3. You will be redirected to your profile page.

How to Login

>
  1. From the navigation bar, go to the Login / Register page.
  2. Enter your username and password and click Log In.
  3. You will be redirected to your profile page.

How to Logout

>
  1. Once logged in, just click in Log Out on the Navigation Bar if you which to leave the session.

How to Create Meals

>
  1. From your profile page, click the plus button or from the navigation bar, go to the Build Meal page.
  2. Enter a descriptive name to your meal (example: Pasta).
  3. Select a picture for your meal (optional).
  4. Select the meal category.
  5. Click on each dropdown section.
  6. Select your servings from the selection list.
  7. Clink on the ADD button.

How to View / Edit Meals

>
  1. From your profile page, click on the Meal you wish to see the details.
  2. The contents of the meal will show.
  3. To Edit the meal, click on the edit button.
  4. Make your updates.
  5. Click on the UPDATE button.

How to Delete Meals

>
  1. From your profile page, click on the minus button of the Meal you wish to delete.
  2. Confirm the deletion.

How to Create Weekly Plan

>
  1. From the navigation bar, got to Plan Week page.
  2. Using the arrows selectors, navigate to your desired week.
  3. Click on the day you wish to plan the Menu.
  4. Select the Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner and Snack from your created Meals for the day.
  5. Click on Submit.

How to Generate Groceries List

>
  1. From the navigation bar, got to Plan Week page.
  2. Using the arrows selectors, navigate to your desired week.
  3. Click on the Get Groceries button.
  4. The groceries list for the week will be generated on a new tab.
